About SOP

A "School of Programming" is an educational institution or program focused on teaching
various programming languages, software development practices, and computer science
fundamentals. It aims to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel
in the tech industry, fostering critical thinking, problem-solving abilities, and
innovation through coding and software development.
